My Role as a UX Researcher

Design Thinking process, wireframing, iteration, testing, presentation.

Visitors to the Colorado Wildlife website often struggle to find the information they need due to difficult navigation and outdated content. Mobile users find the site particularly challenging, and the lack of interactive features makes learning about wildlife conservation less engaging.
The new Colorado Wildlife website will be a welcoming and engaging space for everyone who cares about wildlife. With easier navigation and up-to-date information, finding what you need will be simple and enjoyable. Interactive maps, videos, and virtual tours will bring the beauty and importance of wildlife conservation to life.Â
